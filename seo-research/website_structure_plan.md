`[Value Proposition] [Primary Keyword] [CTA]`

`[Primary Keyword] [Location]`

### Content Writing Rules:
1. **Keyword Placement:**
   - Primary keyword in first 100 words
   - H1, first paragraph, conclusion
   - Natural distribution throughout

   - Use related terms (LSI keywords)
   - Synonyms and variations
   - Industry terminology

3. **User Intent:**
   - Commercial: Focus on benefits, features, pricing
   - Transactional: Clear CTAs, pricing, contact info
   - Informational: Educational, comprehensive

   - Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
   - Bullet points and lists
   - Subheadings for scannability
   - Images every 300 words
