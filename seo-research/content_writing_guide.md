## KEYWORD INTEGRATION RULES

### Primary Keyword Placement
1. **Title Tag:** First 3 words
2. **H1:** Include primary keyword
3. **First 100 words:** Include primary keyword naturally
4. **First paragraph:** Include primary keyword
5. **Conclusion:** Include primary keyword
6. **URL:** Include primary keyword

### Keyword Density
- **Primary keyword:** 1-2% (appears 10-20 times in 1,000 words)
- **Secondary keywords:** 0.5-1% each
- **Related terms (LSI):** Use naturally throughout

### Example: Pellet Mill Dubai Page

**Title:** "Pellet Mill Dubai | Gear Drive & Belt Driven Pellet Mills UAE | Pacific Unity"

**H1:** "Pellet Mills for Feed Production in Dubai & UAE"

**First Paragraph:**
"Looking for a reliable pellet mill in Dubai? Pacific Unity MEA offers premium gear drive and belt driven pellet mills for feed production in the UAE and Middle East. Our pellet mills are designed for efficiency, durability, and optimal feed quality."

**Keywords to include naturally:**
- pellet mill Dubai (primary)
- pellet mill UAE
- gear drive pellet mill
- belt driven pellet mill
- feed production
- animal feed pellet mill
- pellet mill price
- automatic pellet mill

## SEO ELEMENT CHECKLIST

### Title Tag
- [ ] 50-60 characters
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 3 words
- [ ] Brand name at end
- [ ] Unique (not duplicated)
- [ ] Compelling (includes benefit/value)

**Formula:** `[Primary Keyword] | [Secondary Keyword] | [Brand]`

**Example:** "Pellet Mill Dubai | Gear Drive Pellet Mills UAE | Pacific Unity"

### Meta Description
- [ ] 150-160 characters
- [ ] Includes primary keyword
- [ ] Includes value proposition
- [ ] Includes CTA
- [ ] Unique (not duplicated)

**Formula:** `[Value Prop] [Primary Keyword] [CTA]`

**Example:** "Premium pellet mills for feed production in Dubai & UAE. Gear drive & belt driven models. Free consultation. Contact Pacific Unity MEA."

### H1 Tag
- [ ] One H1 per page
- [ ] Includes primary keyword
- [ ] 50-70 characters
- [ ] Clear and descriptive

**Formula:** `[Primary Keyword] [Location/Context]`

**Example:** "Pellet Mills for Feed Production in Dubai & UAE"

### H2-H6 Tags
- [ ] H2s include secondary keywords
- [ ] Logical hierarchy (H2 → H3 → H4)
- [ ] Descriptive and scannable
- [ ] Break up content sections

**Example H2s:**
- "Types of Pellet Mills"
- "Technical Specifications"
- "Applications of Pellet Mills"
- "Pellet Mill Pricing in UAE"
- "Installation & Maintenance"

### Image Alt Text
- [ ] Descriptive
- [ ] Includes relevant keyword (if natural)
- [ ] Not keyword stuffed
- [ ] Describes what's in the image

**Examples:**
- ✅ "Gear drive pellet mill for feed production in Dubai"
- ✅ "Complete feed mill installation in UAE"
- ❌ "pellet mill Dubai pellet mill UAE pellet mill"

### Internal Links
- [ ] 3-5 internal links per page
- [ ] Anchor text is descriptive
- [ ] Links to relevant pages
- [ ] Mix of exact match, partial match, branded

**Anchor Text Distribution:**
- 30% Exact match: "pellet mill Dubai"
- 40% Partial match: "gear drive pellet mill"
- 20% Branded: "Pacific Unity pellet mills"
- 10% Generic: "learn more", "view products"

### Schema Markup
- [ ] Product pages: Product schema
- [ ] Service pages: Service schema
- [ ] Article pages: Article schema
- [ ] FAQ pages: FAQPage schema
- [ ] Organization: Organization schema (on all pages)

## CONTENT WRITING BEST PRACTICES

### Readability
- **Short paragraphs:** 2-3 sentences max
- **Short sentences:** 15-20 words average
- **Bullet points:** Use for lists
- **Subheadings:** Every 300-400 words
- **White space:** Don't cram content

### Tone & Voice
- **Professional but approachable**
- **Confident but not arrogant**
- **Helpful and informative**
- **Industry expertise without jargon overload**

### Content Structure
1. **Hook:** Grab attention in first sentence
2. **Value:** Explain what they'll learn/get
3. **Details:** Provide comprehensive information
4. **Proof:** Case studies, testimonials, certifications
5. **CTA:** Clear call-to-action

### Keyword Usage
- **Natural integration:** Don't force keywords
- **Synonyms:** Use related terms (LSI keywords)
- **Variations:** Use different forms (singular/plural, verb forms)
- **Context:** Keywords should fit the context

### Examples

**❌ Bad (Keyword Stuffing):**
"Looking for a pellet mill Dubai? Our pellet mill Dubai is the best pellet mill Dubai. Buy pellet mill Dubai from Pacific Unity pellet mill Dubai."

**✅ Good (Natural):**
"Looking for a reliable pellet mill in Dubai? Pacific Unity MEA offers premium pellet mills for feed production in the UAE. Our gear drive and belt driven pellet mills are designed for efficiency and durability, making them ideal for feed mills across Dubai and the Middle East."

## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### SEO Mistakes
- ❌ Keyword stuffing
- ❌ Duplicate content
- ❌ Missing alt text on images
- ❌ Broken internal links
- ❌ Missing schema markup
- ❌ Duplicate title tags
- ❌ Missing meta descriptions

### Content Mistakes
- ❌ Too technical (jargon overload)
- ❌ Too generic (no specific value)
- ❌ No clear structure
- ❌ Missing CTAs
- ❌ No proof/trust signals
- ❌ Poor readability

### Writing Mistakes
- ❌ Grammar and spelling errors
- ❌ Inconsistent tone
- ❌ Missing facts/figures
- ❌ No visual breaks (walls of text)
- ❌ Weak introductions
- ❌ No clear conclusion
